Amsterdam. Where it'll average between 22.1 °C (72 °F) and 30 °C (86 °F) on an average summer day. Rich with history. With more than 100 kilometers of canals, the best way to get around the city. The canal system dates back to the 17th century when the Dutch capital. Today these waterways are used for everything from daily commutes to sightseeing. Many of the world's largest companies are based in Amsterdam or have established their European headquarters in the city, such as leading technology companies Uber, Netflix and Tesla. Amsterdam also houses many museums and shops that equally share the history and culture of amsterdam in their own particular way. Cycling is key to the city's character, and there are numerous biking paths and lanes spread throughout the entire city. Amsterdam has been repurposing and narrowing roads for the purpose of making the city more sustainable. All while putting everything anyone may need within walking distance so no one would even need a car unless it was for work. Although the roads aren't wide, Amsterdam loves to throw festivals and events. Whether it be a celebration for Canabis or Gay Pride. This makes Amsterdam a must visit on yourTo Do List!
